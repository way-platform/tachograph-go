3.   REQUIREMENTS APPLICABLE TO THE GNSS RECEIVER OF TRANSITIONAL TACHOGRAPHS

TRA_001  Transitional vehicle units shall include a GNSS receiver capable of using OSNMA available during its public test phase.

TRA_002  Appendix 12 requirements apply to the GNSS receiver included in transitional vehicle units, with the following interpretations:

— 
SIS ICD and OSNMA Receiver Guidelines referred to are the documents available for the public test phase:
— 
Galileo Open Service Navigation Message Authentication (OSNMA) User ICD for the Test Phase, Issue 1.0, November 2021,
— 
Galileo Open Service Navigation Message Authentication (OSNMA) Receiver Guidelines for the Test Phase, Issue 1.0, November 2021,
— 
OSNMA is the service available during the public test phase,
— 
SIS is the Signal In Space available during the public test phase.

TRA_003  The GNSS receiver included in transitional vehicle units shall be so designed that after an update of its software, applied through a vehicle unit software update, it fully complies with Annex 12 requirements, using OSNMA available during its operational phase.
