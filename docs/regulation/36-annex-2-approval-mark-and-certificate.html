ANNEX II: APPROVAL MARK AND CERTIFICATE

I.   APPROVAL MARK

1. The approval mark shall be made up of:

(a)

a rectangle, within which shall be placed the letter ‘e’ followed by a distinguishing number or letter for the country which has issued the approval in accordance with the following conventional signs:

Bulgaria 34,
Czech Republic 8,
Denmark 18,

Malta MT,
Netherlands 4,
Austria 12,
Poland 20,
Portugal 21,
Romania 19,
Slovenia 26,
Slovakia 27,
Finland 17,
Sweden 5,
United Kingdom 11,

and

▼M1

(b)
an approval number corresponding to the number of the approval certificate drawn up for the prototype of the recording equipment or the record sheet or the tachograph card, placed at any point within the immediate proximity of that rectangle.

▼C1

2. The approval mark shall be shown on the descriptive plaque of each set of equipment and on each record sheet and on each tachograph card. It must be indelible and must always remain clearly legible.

3. The dimensions of the approval mark drawn below ( 14 ) are expressed in millimetres, these dimensions being minima. The ratios between the dimensions must be maintained.
